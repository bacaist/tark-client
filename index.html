Frequently
Asked questions

What is Tark?

Tark is an online education platform for students who want to improve themselves. We help you solve difficult questions and understand them better. Thousands of our instructors are ready to give you live lessons. We want to reinforce your knowledge with thousands of offline content.

How can I use it?

Using Tark is quite simple. You can install our application on your smartphone or log in to our web application from your computer.

Is a free usage available?

Yes! When you register, we deliver a 14-day trial package. You can cancel your subscription before your trial package runs out. In this case, you will not be charged any fees.

What can I do when I have a problem?

Never hesitate to write to us :) We will always do our best to solve your problems. To contact us, it is enough to send an e-mail to info@edutark.com. We will get back to you as soon as possible.!

What do you need to know?

Whether you're stumped on geometry or SAT practice, there’s no question too big or too small for Tark.
